Genres

Explore the diverse and rich music genres of Egypt.

Arabic Classical Music

The traditional music of Egypt, similar to other Middle Eastern classical music, dating back to the 7th century. Influenced by Persian, Indian, and Greek music, it features distinctive quarter notes, adding a unique flavor cherished by Egyptians.

Nubian Music

Originating from the Nubian people in southern Egypt, this genre traditionally uses the daf drum and hand clapping. Pioneered by Mohamed Mounir and Hamza el-Din, it integrates traditional sounds with urban influences.

Shaabi

A form of Egyptian street and pop music, Shaabi is widely popular and often blended with Western styles. Ahmed Adaweyah is a key figure in the genre, known for bringing its sound to mainstream popularity. It is one of the most popular music of Egypt
